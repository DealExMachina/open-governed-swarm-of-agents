MERIDIAN HOLDINGS -- CONSOLIDATED Q1 2025 FINANCIAL SUMMARY

Prepared by: Group CFO Office
Date: 2025-04-18
Valid period: Q1 2025 (January 1 -- March 31, 2025)
Classification: INTERNAL -- BOARD MATERIALS

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Meridian Holdings reports consolidated revenue of EUR 127.4M for Q1 2025, representing 12% year-over-year growth from Q1 2024 (EUR 113.8M). The Group comprises three operating subsidiaries:

- Alpha Industrials (manufacturing): EUR 58.2M revenue
- Beta Services (consulting & professional services): EUR 41.6M revenue
- Gamma Digital (SaaS & data analytics): EUR 27.6M revenue

CONSOLIDATED P&L HIGHLIGHTS

Total consolidated revenue: EUR 127.4M
Inter-company eliminations: EUR (4.8M)
Net external revenue: EUR 122.6M
Cost of revenue: EUR 71.5M
Gross profit: EUR 51.1M (consolidated gross margin: 41.7%)
Operating expenses: EUR 34.2M
EBITDA: EUR 16.9M (EBITDA margin: 13.8%)

WORKING CAPITAL

Group cash position as of March 31, 2025: EUR 42.3M
Net working capital: EUR 28.7M
Days sales outstanding (DSO): 54 days

HEADCOUNT

Total group FTEs: 1,247 across all subsidiaries.

OUTLOOK

Management reaffirms full-year 2025 guidance of EUR 520M consolidated revenue and EBITDA margin of 14-15%. Q2 pipeline visibility is strong across all three subsidiaries, with particular momentum in Gamma Digital's enterprise SaaS bookings.

NOTES

These figures are based on subsidiary management reports received April 12-15, 2025. Figures are unaudited and subject to revision upon completion of inter-company reconciliation. The Ernst & Young interim review is scheduled for June 2025.

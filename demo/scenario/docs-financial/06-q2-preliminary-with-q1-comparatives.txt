Q2 SUBSIDIARY PERFORMANCE

Alpha Industrials: EUR 63.8M (Q2) vs EUR 60.5M (Q1 restated). The Nordstern Q2 milestone was partially delivered; EUR 0.6M of the EUR 1.2M provision has been released. Remaining provision: EUR 0.6M.

Beta Services: EUR 44.7M (Q2) vs EUR 43.9M (Q1). The BfS change orders from Q1 have now been signed (EUR 1.9M). The Berlin restructuring is complete; no further restructuring charges expected.

Gamma Digital: EUR 30.7M (Q2) vs EUR 27.0M (Q1, group methodology). ARR grew to EUR 106.2M. Daimler Truck AG renewed for 2 years but at a 12% discount to the previous rate, reducing ARR contribution from EUR 9.2M to EUR 8.1M. Management characterizes this as "in line with enterprise renewal norms" but the discount was not anticipated in our Q1 outlook.

HEADCOUNT

Group FTEs as of June 30: 1,298 (was 1,247 at March 31 per the original Q1 report; however, restated Q1 headcount using the revised contractor methodology is 1,271, making the Q/Q increase 27 FTEs rather than 51).

CASH POSITION

Group cash: EUR 38.7M (was EUR 42.3M at Q1-end). The decrease primarily reflects the Augsburg line capex and the Gamma Digital capital injection, partially offset by operating cash flow. We note that the Gamma Digital EUR 5.0M injection is classified as equity at the subsidiary level but as an inter-company loan at group level. This discrepancy has been flagged for the interim review.

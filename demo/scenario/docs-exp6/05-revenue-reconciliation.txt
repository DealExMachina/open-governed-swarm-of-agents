Prepared by: Joint Finance Working Group
Date: 2025-10-22
Classification: CONFIDENTIAL

RECONCILIATION RESULTS

Following collaborative review between MedCore's CFO and the external audit team, the revenue discrepancy has been partially resolved:

1. REVENUE: The correct audited figure is EUR 76M, not EUR 85M or EUR 72M.
   - The external audit's EUR 72M figure excluded EUR 4M in legitimately recognized maintenance revenue that qualifies for immediate recognition under IFRS 15.
   - MedCore's initial EUR 85M included EUR 9M in improperly recognized multi-year implementation fees.
   - Net adjustment: EUR -9M from initial claim.

2. GROWTH RATE: Adjusted growth is 31% YoY after applying consistent recognition methodology to both years.

3. GROSS MARGIN: Agreed margin is 64% after reclassifying EUR 3M in hosting costs (not EUR 5M as initially claimed by auditors — EUR 2M was correctly classified as R&D for a dedicated research cluster).

4. CLIENT COUNT: Agreed count is 108 active clients. Fourteen of the disputed 26 accounts have signed paid contracts effective Q4 2025.

This resolves the revenue, growth, margin, and client count contradictions with agreed reconciled figures.

REVISED VALUATION

Based on reconciled financials, proposed valuation range: EUR 550M-580M.

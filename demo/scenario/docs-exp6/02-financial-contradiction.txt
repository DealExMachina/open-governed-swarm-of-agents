PROJECT ATLAS — INDEPENDENT FINANCIAL REVIEW

Prepared by: External Audit Partners
Date: 2025-10-01
Classification: CONFIDENTIAL

FINDINGS

The independent financial review reveals significant discrepancies with the initial assessment:

1. REVENUE DISCREPANCY: Audited revenue for FY 2024 is EUR 72M, not EUR 85M as previously reported. The EUR 13M difference is attributed to multi-year contract revenue that was recognized upfront rather than over the contract period under IFRS 15.

2. GROWTH RATE: Adjusted year-over-year growth is 28%, not 38%. Prior year figures were also subject to the same recognition methodology.

3. GROSS MARGIN: Actual gross margin after reclassifying hosting costs is 61%, not 68%. Approximately EUR 5M in infrastructure costs were incorrectly classified as R&D expenditure.

4. CLIENT COUNT: Active paying clients number 94, not 120. Twenty-six accounts are in pilot or free-tier status and should not be counted as enterprise clients.

These findings contradict the initial assessment claims regarding revenue, growth, margins, and client count.

RECOMMENDATION

Valuation should be revised downward. Proposed range: EUR 480M-520M based on adjusted multiples.

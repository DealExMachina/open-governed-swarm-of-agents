PROJECT ONBOARDING -- AGRIVOLT SAS

Date: 2025-02-15
Project: AgriVolt Provence Agrivoltaic Park
Sponsor: AgriVolt SAS (France)
Category: A -- Renewable energy (Solar PV, agrivoltaic)
Taxonomy NACE: D35.11

Project description:
- Location: Bouches-du-Rhone, Provence, France
- Capacity: 25 MW (elevated structure, agrivoltaic design allowing continued agricultural use)
- EPC contractor: Voltalia S.A.
- Grid connection: RTE, expected Q3 2025
- Agricultural use: Lavender cultivation beneath panels (maintained by local cooperative)
- Estimated annual generation: 35 GWh
- Estimated avoided emissions: 9,800 tCO2e/year (French grid factor 0.28 tCO2e/MWh)

Financial terms:
- Total project cost: EUR 30,000,000
- Bond allocation requested: EUR 25,000,000
- Drawdown schedule: 50% at financial close (Mar 2025), 50% at COD (Q3 2025)
- Revenue: Feed-in premium under French CRE5 tender at EUR 62/MWh

Green Bond Committee assessment:
- Substantial contribution: Agrivoltaic solar, life-cycle emissions 30 gCO2e/kWh. Aligned.
- DNSH: Environmental impact assessment approved. Agricultural co-use preserves land utility (no LULUCF conflict).
- Social safeguards: French labor code. Cooperative partnership agreement ensures local employment.

Decision: APPROVED for allocation. This additional Category A project raises total allocated to EUR 140,200,000 and improves Taxonomy alignment.

Claim: AgriVolt approved for EUR 25M. Category A, fully Taxonomy-aligned. Goal: Improve overall Taxonomy alignment by adding to renewable energy allocation.

TERM SHEET -- EuroVert Capital Green Bond Fund I

Issuer: EuroVert Capital S.a r.l., a Luxembourg-domiciled special purpose vehicle.
Bond type: Senior unsecured green bond.
Target size: EUR 250,000,000.
Coupon: Fixed 4.25% per annum, payable semi-annually.
Maturity: 7 years from issuance date.
Use of proceeds: Financing and refinancing of Eligible Green Projects per the EuroVert Green Bond Framework aligned with ICMA Green Bond Principles 2021.
Eligible categories: Renewable energy (solar PV, onshore wind), energy efficiency (building retrofits, industrial process), clean transportation (EV charging infrastructure).
Taxonomy alignment target: Minimum 85% of net proceeds allocated to EU Taxonomy-aligned activities.
Listing: Luxembourg Stock Exchange, Green Bond segment.
Governing law: Luxembourg law.
Arranger: Banque Verte S.A.
External reviewer: Sustainalytics (Second Party Opinion, pre-issuance).

Key dates:
- Framework publication: 2024-09-15
- SPO delivery: 2024-10-01 (expected)
- Pricing: 2024-10-15 (expected)
- Settlement: 2024-10-22 (expected)
- First allocation report: 2025-04-30
- EUGBS application date: 2024-12-21

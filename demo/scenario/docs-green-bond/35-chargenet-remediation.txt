REMEDIATION PLAN -- CHARGENET B.V.

Date: 2026-01-15
Subject: ChargeNet utilization improvement and deployment completion

Following the utilization alert (June 2025), ChargeNet has implemented the following measures:

1. DEPLOYMENT COMPLETION:
- Remaining 320 charging points installed (total: 2,500)
- Grid connections completed for all units (Stedin backlog resolved December 2025)
- All 2,500 points now operational

2. UTILIZATION IMPROVEMENT:
- Relocated 180 underperforming suburban chargers to higher-traffic locations (urban centers, highway rest areas)
- Partnership with fleet operators (PostNL, DHL Express) for dedicated fleet charging
- Dynamic pricing introduced (lower off-peak rates to improve load factor)

Updated metrics (Q4 2025):
- Average utilization: 11.8% (up from 8.2% in June 2025)
- Revenue per point: EUR 2,650/year (up from EUR 1,840/year; business plan target: EUR 3,200/year)
- Estimated annual sessions: 425,000 (up from 285,000)

The utilization improvement narrows the gap to business plan projections. Full network deployment and fleet partnerships are expected to sustain the improvement trend.

Claim: ChargeNet fully deployed (2,500 points operational). Utilization improved to 11.8% (from 8.2%). Still below business plan but trending positively. Goal: Reach 14% utilization by Q2 2026 through fleet partnerships and dynamic pricing.

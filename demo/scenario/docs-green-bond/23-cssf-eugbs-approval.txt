CSSF DECISION -- EUGBS DESIGNATION

Date: 2025-05-20
Reference: CSSF/GB/2025/0018

The Commission de Surveillance du Secteur Financier (CSSF), acting as competent authority under Regulation (EU) 2023/2631, hereby confirms that:

Bond: EuroVert Capital Green Bond Fund I (XS2987654321)
Issuer: EuroVert Capital S.a r.l.

is designated as a EUROPEAN GREEN BOND (EuGB) with effect from 2025-05-20.

The designation is granted on the basis that:
1. The green bond factsheet has been reviewed by a registered external reviewer (Sustainalytics, ESMA/ER/2025/0042).
2. The use of proceeds is consistent with Article 4 of the Regulation.
3. The issuer has committed to allocating at least 85% of net proceeds to EU Taxonomy-aligned activities.
4. Annual allocation and impact reporting obligations are acknowledged.

Ongoing obligations:
- Annual allocation report with external reviewer verification
- Annual impact report
- Notification of any material change to the green bond factsheet or use of proceeds
- CSSF retains supervisory powers under Article 44

This designation does not constitute an endorsement of the creditworthiness or investment merit of the bond.

Claim: EUGBS designation obtained from CSSF. Bond is now officially a European Green Bond. Goal: Maintain compliance with ongoing EUGBS obligations including annual reporting and external review.

EUROVERT GREEN BOND FRAMEWORK

Version 1.0 -- Published 2024-09-15

1. USE OF PROCEEDS

Net proceeds will be allocated to Eligible Green Projects in three categories:

Category A -- Renewable energy (target 50-60%):
- Solar PV installations (>10 MW capacity)
- Onshore wind farms (EU-located, Taxonomy NACE D35.11)
Screening criteria: Life-cycle GHG emissions below 100 gCO2e/kWh.

Category B -- Energy efficiency (target 25-35%):
- Building renovation achieving minimum 30% primary energy demand reduction
- Industrial process efficiency (NACE C10-C32)
Screening criteria: Primary energy demand at least 30% below national nZEB standard for new buildings; or at least 30% reduction vs baseline for renovations.

Category C -- Clean transportation (target 10-15%):
- EV charging infrastructure (public and semi-public)
- Zero-emission public transport vehicles
Screening criteria: Zero direct tailpipe emissions for vehicles; grid-connected for charging.

2. ALIGNMENT

This framework targets alignment with:
- ICMA Green Bond Principles (2021 edition)
- EU Taxonomy Regulation (2020/852), Climate Delegated Act
- EU Green Bond Standard (Regulation 2023/2631) upon application (December 2024)

At issuance, the framework is ICMA GBP-aligned. After EUGBS application date (21 December 2024), the issuer intends to seek designation as a European Green Bond, subject to external reviewer registration and factsheet requirements.

3. PROJECT EVALUATION

An internal Green Bond Committee (GBC) evaluates and selects projects. The GBC comprises the CIO, Head of ESG, and an independent sustainability advisor. Projects must satisfy:
- Substantial contribution to climate change mitigation (Taxonomy Art. 10)
- Do No Significant Harm to other environmental objectives (Art. 17)
- Minimum social safeguards (Art. 18)

4. MANAGEMENT OF PROCEEDS

Unallocated proceeds held in cash, short-term sovereign bonds (EEA), or green money market instruments. Tracking via internal ledger reconciled quarterly.

5. REPORTING

Annual allocation report (audited) and impact report (estimated avoided emissions, installed capacity). External review: pre-issuance SPO (Sustainalytics), post-allocation verification (to be appointed).

Claim: Framework is ICMA GBP-aligned at issuance. Goal: Achieve EUGBS designation after regulatory application date.

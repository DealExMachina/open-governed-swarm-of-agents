FRAMEWORK UPDATE -- EUROVERT GREEN BOND FRAMEWORK V1.1

Date: 2025-01-25
Changes from v1.0:

1. CATEGORY B -- ENERGY EFFICIENCY (updated):
- Building renovations: Mandatory Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) before and after renovation, documenting the improvement. The post-renovation EPC must show at least class B or a two-class improvement.
- Industrial process efficiency: Added DNSH water use disclosure requirement per Taxonomy Delegated Act Annex I, Section 7.2.
- Added: Circular economy impact assessment for building demolition waste (target: 70% reuse/recycling by weight).

2. EUGBS TRANSITION (new section):
- The issuer commits to applying for European Green Bond designation under Regulation 2023/2631 once all preconditions are met.
- A green bond factsheet per EUGBS Annex I will be prepared and submitted to the external reviewer.
- Post-allocation verification by a registered external reviewer will be obtained.

3. PROJECT SUBSTITUTION (clarified):
- Any project that becomes Taxonomy-ineligible (e.g., fails updated TSC, or is no longer operational) must be substituted within 90 days.
- Substitution disclosed in the next quarterly allocation report.

4. REPORTING (enhanced):
- Annual allocation report to include Taxonomy alignment percentage per category, with external assurance.
- Impact report methodology aligned with ICMA Harmonized Framework for Impact Reporting (2024 edition).

External reviewer confirmation: Sustainalytics has confirmed notification to ESMA under the EUGBS transitional provisions. Updated SPO expected Q1 2025.

Claim: Framework v1.1 addresses all SPO reservations (EPC, DNSH water, circular economy). Goal: Obtain updated SPO and prepare for EUGBS factsheet.

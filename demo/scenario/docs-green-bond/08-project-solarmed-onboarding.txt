PROJECT ONBOARDING -- SOLARMED S.R.L.

Date: 2024-11-05
Project: SolarMed Puglia Solar Park
Sponsor: SolarMed S.r.l. (Italy)
Category: A -- Renewable energy (Solar PV)
Taxonomy NACE: D35.11

Project description:
- Location: Puglia region, southern Italy
- Capacity: 45 MW (single-axis tracking)
- EPC contractor: Enel Green Power (turnkey)
- Grid connection: Terna S.p.A., expected Q2 2025
- Lifetime: 30 years
- Estimated annual generation: 72 GWh
- Estimated avoided emissions: 28,800 tCO2e/year (grid emission factor 0.4 tCO2e/MWh)

Financial terms:
- Total project cost: EUR 42,000,000
- Bond allocation requested: EUR 38,000,000
- Drawdown schedule: 40% at financial close (Nov 2024), 30% at mechanical completion (Feb 2025), 30% at COD (Q2 2025)
- Revenue: 15-year PPA with Italian utility at EUR 58/MWh (fixed, CPI-indexed)

Green Bond Committee assessment:
- Substantial contribution: Solar PV, life-cycle emissions estimated at 25 gCO2e/kWh (well below 100 gCO2e/kWh threshold).
- DNSH: Environmental impact assessment (EIA) approved by Puglia Region (2024-06-12). No Natura 2000 sites within 5 km.
- Social safeguards: EPC contractor subject to Italian labor code. ILO conventions respected.

Decision: APPROVED for allocation. First drawdown: EUR 15,200,000 on 2024-11-15.

Claim: SolarMed project approved for EUR 38M allocation. Taxonomy-aligned (Category A). Goal: Achieve COD by Q2 2025 and full drawdown.

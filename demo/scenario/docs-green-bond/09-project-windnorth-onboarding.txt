PROJECT ONBOARDING -- WINDNORTH APS

Date: 2024-11-12
Project: WindNorth Jutland Onshore Wind Farm
Sponsor: WindNorth ApS (Denmark)
Category: A -- Renewable energy (Onshore wind)
Taxonomy NACE: D35.11

Project description:
- Location: North Jutland, Denmark
- Capacity: 80 MW (20 x Vestas V150 turbines)
- Status: OPERATIONAL since June 2024
- Annual generation (actual): 210 GWh (capacity factor 30%)
- Estimated avoided emissions: 29,400 tCO2e/year (Danish grid factor 0.14 tCO2e/MWh)

Financial terms:
- Total project cost: EUR 95,000,000 (fully funded at COD)
- Bond allocation requested: EUR 62,000,000 (refinancing of existing bank facility)
- Single drawdown at allocation
- Revenue: Corporate PPA with Orsted at EUR 42/MWh (12 years, DKK-linked)

Green Bond Committee assessment:
- Substantial contribution: Onshore wind, life-cycle emissions 8 gCO2e/kWh.
- DNSH: Danish Environmental Protection Agency approval (2023-03-15). Avian impact study completed; no endangered species conflict.
- Social safeguards: Danish labor standards. Community benefit agreement in place.

Decision: APPROVED for allocation. Drawdown: EUR 62,000,000 on 2024-11-20.

Claim: WindNorth approved for EUR 62M refinancing allocation. Operational asset with proven generation. Goal: Refinance existing facility and report allocation.

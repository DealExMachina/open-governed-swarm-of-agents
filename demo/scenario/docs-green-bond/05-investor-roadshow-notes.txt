INVESTOR ROADSHOW NOTES -- CONFIDENTIAL

Date: 2024-10-08 to 2024-10-11
Participants: EuroVert Asset Management, Banque Verte (arranger), 14 institutional investors.

Key investor questions and responses:

Q1 (Nordic pension fund): "What is your plan for EUGBS compliance after December 2024?"
A: The issuer intends to seek EUGBS designation once the regulation applies. The framework already targets 85% Taxonomy alignment. External reviewer appointment under EUGBS will be addressed in Q1 2025.

Q2 (German insurer): "Category B -- Sustainalytics flagged partial alignment. How will you address this?"
A: We are updating the framework to include mandatory EPC documentation for all building renovation projects and DNSH water disclosure for industrial projects. Framework v1.1 expected by end of November.

Q3 (French asset manager): "Pipeline visibility -- what projects are you targeting?"
A: Current pipeline includes:
- SolarMed S.r.l. (Italy): 45 MW solar PV, EUR 38M, construction start Q4 2024
- WindNorth ApS (Denmark): 80 MW onshore wind, EUR 62M, operational since June 2024
- RetroFit GmbH (Germany): 12 commercial building renovations, EUR 28M, works 2024-2026
- ChargeNet B.V. (Netherlands): 2,500 public EV charging points, EUR 18M, rollout 2024-2025

Q4 (UK fund): "What is the expected green premium (greenium)?"
A: Initial price guidance at MS+145bps; comparable conventional bonds at MS+155-160bps, suggesting a greenium of 10-15bps.

Q5 (Swiss bank): "How do you handle project substitution if a project becomes ineligible?"
A: The Green Bond Committee has authority to substitute projects, subject to the same eligibility criteria. Any substitution will be disclosed in the next allocation report and subject to external review.

Investor indication of interest: EUR 380M (1.52x oversubscribed).

Claim: Strong investor demand at 1.52x oversubscription. Pipeline of 4 identified projects totalling EUR 146M. Goal: Price at MS+145bps and achieve full allocation.

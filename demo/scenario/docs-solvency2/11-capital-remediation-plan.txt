1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This Capital Remediation Plan ("the Plan") is submitted to the
Bundesanstalt fur Finanzdienstleistungsaufsicht (BaFin) in response
to the supervisory letter dated 22 May 2026 (Ref. VA 44-I 4000-2026/
0087). The Plan sets out concrete measures to restore Meridian's SCR
ratio from its current level of approximately 128% to the internal
target of 155% within 6 months (by 31 December 2026).

Note: The internal target has been raised from 135% to 155% by Board
decision on 15 July 2026, reflecting the increased regulatory
uncertainty and the need for a larger buffer.

2. CURRENT POSITION (AS AT 30 JUNE 2026)

Eligible own funds:        EUR 892,000,000
SCR:                       EUR 689,000,000
SCR ratio:                 129.5% (approximately 130%)
MCR ratio:                 388%
Surplus above SCR:         EUR 203,000,000
Deficit to 155% target:    EUR 176,000,000

3. REMEDIATION MEASURES

MEASURE 1 — SUBORDINATED DEBT ISSUANCE
  Instrument: Tier 2 subordinated notes, 10-year maturity,
              callable after 5 years
  Target amount:        EUR 150,000,000
  Expected coupon:      4.75% (based on current market conditions)
  Lead arrangers:       Deutsche Bank AG, BNP Paribas SA
  Expected completion:  September 2026
  Regulatory treatment: Tier 2 own funds (subject to 50% limit test)
  SCR ratio impact:     +21.8 percentage points

  Tier compliance check:
    Current Tier 2:     EUR 80,000,000
    After issuance:     EUR 230,000,000
    Tier 2 limit (50% of SCR): EUR 344,500,000
    COMPLIANT

MEASURE 2 — DIVIDEND SUSPENSION
  FY2025 final dividend: SUSPENDED (was EUR 40,000,000)
  FY2026 interim dividend: NOT DECLARED
  Capital retained:     EUR 30,000,000 (estimated, net of tax)
  SCR ratio impact:     +4.4 percentage points
  Shareholder communication: Ad hoc announcement on 16 July 2026
    via DGAP, referencing regulatory capital management priorities

MEASURE 3 — INVESTMENT PORTFOLIO DE-RISKING
  Action: Reduce equity allocation from EUR 180,000,000 to
          EUR 120,000,000 (reinvest into investment-grade corporate
          bonds and covered bonds)
  Capital release:      EUR 25,000,000 (reduced market risk charge)
  SCR ratio impact:     +3.6 percentage points
  Expected return drag: approximately EUR 3,000,000 p.a.
  Timeline:             Phased over August-September 2026

MEASURE 4 — REINSURANCE OPTIMIZATION
  Action: Novate EUR 30,000,000 of Swiss Re exposure to Munich Re
  Action: Purchase catastrophe bond protection (EUR 50,000,000 cover)
  Capital release:      EUR 15,000,000 (reduced counterparty and
                        underwriting risk charges)
  SCR ratio impact:     +2.2 percentage points
  Additional cost:      EUR 2,800,000 p.a.
  Timeline:             September 2026

5. KEY RISKS TO THE PLAN

5.1 Market Conditions for Debt Issuance
  Risk: Subordinated debt market closes or spreads widen materially
  Mitigation: Pre-arranged bridge facility of EUR 100M from Deutsche
  Bank AG (term sheet signed 10 July 2026)
  Probability: LOW

5.2 Further Regulatory Changes
  Risk: EIOPA or BaFin issues further guidelines affecting SCR
  Mitigation: Conservative buffer in the 155% target
  Probability: MEDIUM

5.3 Swiss Re Further Downgrade
  Risk: Swiss Re downgraded to BBB+ or below
  Mitigation: Active novation program (Measure 4)
  Probability: MEDIUM (Moody's review still pending)

5.4 Adverse Claims Experience
  Risk: Large loss event depletes own funds
  Mitigation: Catastrophe bond protection (Measure 4)
  Probability: LOW (within normal frequency expectations)

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This report presents the Solvency Capital Requirement calculation for
Meridian Insurance Group AG as at 31 December 2025, computed under the
Solvency II standard formula in accordance with Delegated Regulation
(EU) 2015/35, as amended.

The Group SCR ratio stands at 142%, indicating a comfortable surplus
above regulatory minimums. The Board may rely on this figure for
capital planning and dividend distribution decisions.

4. SCR RATIO

SCR Ratio = 890 / 627 = 142.0%

The minimum regulatory threshold is 100%. The internal target approved
by the Board on 12 November 2025 is 135%. The current ratio provides a
buffer of EUR 263,000,000 above the SCR.

5. TRANSITIONAL MEASURES

Meridian applies the transitional measure on technical provisions under
Art. 308c of Directive 2009/138/EC, as permitted by BaFin approval
letter Ref. VA 44-I 4000-2024/0031 dated 3 March 2024. The transitional
adjustment amounts to EUR 45,000,000, which reduces the technical
provisions accordingly. Without this adjustment, the SCR ratio would
be approximately 135%.

This transitional measure is subject to annual recalculation and
supervisory review. The current allowance of EUR 45,000,000 was
validated by the appointed actuary, Herr Prof. Stefan Bruckner, on
14 December 2025 and approved by the Supervisory Board on 20 December 2025.

6. LOOK-THROUGH APPROACH

Meridian holds EUR 340,000,000 in collective investment undertakings.
The look-through approach under Art. 84 of the Delegated Regulation has
been applied to 78% of fund holdings by value (EUR 265,200,000). For
the remaining 22% (EUR 74,800,000), Meridian applies the mandate-based
approach due to insufficient granularity of fund-level data.

OVERALL COMPLIANCE POSTURE

NorthGrid Utilities maintains compliance programs for NERC CIP standards
CIP-002 through CIP-014. The current aggregate compliance score, based
on the internal weighted compliance index (WCI), stands at 94.0%.

This report summarizes the status following the Q4 2025 internal audit
conducted by Deloitte & Touche LLP (engagement ref: DT-NG-2025-CIP-04).

MEDIUM-RISK FINDINGS (6 total)

CIP-007 — Systems Security Management (3 findings):
  F-007-01: Patch management cycle exceeds 35-day window for 12 relay
            protection devices in South balancing area. Root cause:
            vendor delayed patch qualification testing.
  F-007-02: Two SCADA workstations at Lakeside Central Hub found with
            disabled antivirus agents (discovered during spot check).
  F-007-03: Security event log forwarding failure on 8 BCAs in Coastal
            area EMS segment. Logs not reaching SIEM for 72 hours.

CIP-010 — Configuration Change Management (2 findings):
  F-010-01: 14 firewall rule changes in Central area implemented without
            completed change request documentation.
  F-010-02: Baseline configuration deviation detected on 6 RTUs at
            Granite Falls — firmware version mismatch vs. approved baseline.

CIP-014 — Physical Security (1 finding):
  F-014-01: Perimeter intrusion detection sensor at Harborview station
            intermittently offline (3 occurrences in Q4, total 18 hours).

REMEDIATION PLAN

All 6 findings assigned to remediation owners with target closure dates:
  - CIP-007 findings: remediation due by March 31, 2026
  - CIP-010 findings: remediation due by February 28, 2026
  - CIP-014 finding:  remediation due by January 31, 2026

Quarterly compliance filing to NERC ReliabilityFirst due April 15, 2026.

NOTE: Current 94% WCI score represents a 2-point improvement from Q3 2025
(92%). The compliance team considers this trajectory acceptable, but flags

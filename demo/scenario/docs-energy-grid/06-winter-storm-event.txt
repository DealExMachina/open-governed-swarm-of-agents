TIMELINE OF EVENTS (all times UTC)

February 12, 2026:
  04:00  NWS issues Winter Storm Warning. NorthGrid activates Cold
         Weather Preparedness Plan (NG-OP-412). Ambient temperature: -6C.
  08:00  Temperature drops to -12C. Gas pipeline operator TransCon
         Energy notifies of reduced pipeline pressure on Line 4200
         (serving Central area gas units). Reason cited: "operational
         constraints due to extreme cold."
  11:30  Temperature reaches -15C. Central area gas units Lakeside CC
         Units 1-3 (total 1,200 MW) derate to 65% due to reduced gas
         pressure. Dispatch requests emergency gas from spot market.
  14:00  Temperature: -17C. Gas supply to South area gas peakers
         curtailed entirely. Four peaking units (total 800 MW) forced
         offline: Riverside CT 1-2 (400 MW), Valley Peak CT 1-2 (400 MW).
  15:22  Millstone Nuclear Unit 2 (850 MW) experiences circulating water
         intake screen icing. Unit derates to 600 MW.
  16:45  Total generation lost: 3,200 MW. System load: 10,800 MW.
         Available generation: 9,100 MW. Operating reserve deficit: 1,900 MW.
  16:48  SYSTEM FREQUENCY DROPS. SCADA records frequency nadir of
         59.87 Hz at 16:48:23 UTC. Duration below 59.95 Hz: 47 seconds.
         Under-frequency load shedding (UFLS) Scheme A armed but not
         triggered (threshold: 59.75 Hz).
  16:49  Emergency demand response activated: 1,800 MW of interruptible
         load shed across all four balancing areas.
  16:51  MidAtlantic ISO emergency energy import: 1,200 MW via 345kV ties.
  16:55  Frequency recovered to 59.97 Hz.
  17:10  Frequency stabilized at 59.99 Hz. Emergency conditions continue.

February 13, 2026:
  02:00  Temperature: -18C (event minimum). System load drops to 8,200 MW
         overnight. Emergency imports maintained at 800 MW.
  09:00  Gas pressure partially restored on Line 4200. Lakeside CC Units
         1-2 return to full output. Unit 3 remains derated.
  14:00  Temperature rises to -10C. Valley Peak CT 1 restarted.

February 14, 2026:
  06:00  All generation units restored to service except Riverside CT 2
         (frozen instrumentation, requires maintenance).
  12:00  Emergency conditions terminated. Normal operations resume.

BAL-003 COMPLIANCE NOTE

The frequency excursion to 59.87 Hz (per SCADA) for 47 seconds exceeds
the NorthGrid Frequency Trigger Limit of 59.90 Hz for events lasting
more than 30 seconds. This constitutes a reportable BAL-003 exceedance.

A detailed frequency analysis using PMU data is underway and may revise
the recorded nadir value. See forthcoming document NG-FREQ-2026-02-POST.

IMMEDIATE CORRECTIVE ACTIONS

  1. Emergency gas supply contracts activated with alternate pipeline
  2. Cold weather readiness audit of all gas-dependent generation
  3. Nuclear plant winterization inspection (circulating water systems)
  4. Review of demand response activation procedures and speed
  5. Coordination call with TransCon Energy re: pipeline reliability

NOTE: PMU and SCADA readings may diverge during fast transient events.
SCADA's 2-second averaging window can mask sub-second frequency nadirs.
For compliance reporting purposes, SCADA values are currently used as
the official record per NorthGrid Procedure NG-OP-301.

SETTLEMENT METHODOLOGY

Metering:
  - Revenue-grade meters at all generator interconnection points
  - Telemetry sampling: 15-minute interval reads (aligned to clock)
  - Settlement interval: 5 minutes (interpolated from 15-min reads)
  - Data source: SCADA/EMS telemetry system, validated by meter agents

NOTE: The 15-minute metering interval is a known limitation for
settlement accuracy during fast-changing conditions (e.g., wind ramps,
demand response events). A proposal to upgrade to 4-second telemetry
for settlement-grade metering is under review (NG-PROJ-2026-017).

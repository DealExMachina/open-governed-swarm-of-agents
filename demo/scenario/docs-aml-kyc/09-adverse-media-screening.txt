2. ARTICLE SUMMARY

The Corriere della Sera reports that the Guardia di Finanza (Italian
financial police) has opened a preliminary investigation ("indagine
preliminare") into potential irregularities in the award of municipal
maintenance contracts in Milan's Municipio 4 (Ward 4).

Key facts reported:
  - Marco Castellan (Consigliere Comunale, PD) is "under scrutiny"
    ("sotto esame") in connection with the investigation
  - The investigation concerns EUR 2.7M in maintenance contracts awarded
    to three companies between 2021-2023
  - Allegations include potential conflict of interest and irregular
    tender procedures
  - No formal charges have been filed
  - Marco Castellan's lawyer issued a statement: "My client has acted
    with full transparency and will cooperate fully with authorities"
  - The Procura della Repubblica (Milan Public Prosecutor) confirmed
    the existence of the investigation but declined to comment on details

6. REGULATORY IMPLICATIONS

Per FATF Guidance on PEPs (2013) and 5AMLD Article 20:
  - When a PEP family member becomes subject to criminal investigation,
    enhanced measures must be applied to all related business relationships
  - The bank must assess whether the existing business relationship could
    be used to launder proceeds of the alleged criminal activity
  - Senior management must be informed and approve continuation of the
    relationship

Per German GwG (Geldwaeschegesetz) Section 15(5):
  - Higher risk situations involving PEPs require approval of senior
    management for continuing the business relationship

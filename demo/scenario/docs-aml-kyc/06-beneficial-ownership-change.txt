  Date of Trust Amendment:       2023-10-15

  Date Discovered by Bank:       2024-04-22 (annual review)
  Notification Delay:            190 days (6+ months)

This constitutes a BREACH of the client's contractual notification
obligations and raises concerns about the client's transparency and
willingness to cooperate with AML/KYC requirements.

1. DISCOVERY

During the post-EDD investigation and SAR-related evidence gathering,
the KYC team requested an updated confirmation of the CastleTech BVI Ltd
shareholder register from the BVI registered agent (Trident Trust Company).

The response, received 2024-11-12, revealed a MATERIAL DISCREPANCY with
the corporate structure declared at onboarding.

2. DISCREPANCY DETAIL

  DECLARED AT ONBOARDING (CDD-2023-CAST-0002, dated 2023-03-15):
    CastleTech BVI Ltd — 100% owned by Castellan Holdings S.a.r.l.
    Sole Director: Viktor Castellan

  CURRENT SHAREHOLDER REGISTER (Trident Trust confirmation, 2024-11-12):
    CastleTech BVI Ltd:
      70% — Castellan Holdings S.a.r.l.
      30% — Cerberus Consulting AG (Switzerland, CHE-412.876.553)
    Directors: Viktor Castellan, Luca Ferretti (appointed 2024-04-01)

  CHANGE DATE: Share transfer registered 2024-04-01
  NOTIFICATION TO EUROBANK: NONE

3. CONTRADICTION ANALYSIS

This discovery creates MULTIPLE contradictions with existing records:

  (a) CDD-2023-CAST-0002: Stated CastleTech BVI was "100% held by
      Castellan Holdings S.a.r.l." — this was accurate at onboarding
      but is no longer true as of 2024-04-01.

  (b) CDD-2023-CAST-0001: UBO analysis based on Viktor 78% / Elena 22%
      ownership of Castellan Holdings, which held 100% of CastleTech BVI.
      With 30% of BVI now held externally, the effective group ownership
      and UBO calculations must be revised.

  (c) TXN-2023-CAST-0001: BVI dividends (EUR 500K quarterly) were
      profiled as intra-group transfers. With 30% external ownership,
      dividend flows may be subject to third-party claims.

  (d) EDD-2024-CAST-0001: Cerberus was assessed as a consulting
      counterparty. It is now revealed to be a 30% SHAREHOLDER of
      a group subsidiary — fundamentally different relationship.

  (e) TXN-ALERT-2024-0287: The EUR 3.3M in "consulting payments" to
      Cerberus must now be re-evaluated. Were these genuine consulting
      fees, or were they connected to the share acquisition? The 30%
      stake in CastleTech BVI has an estimated value of EUR 3-5M based
      on BVI entity revenue — closely matching the payment amounts.

4. CIRCULAR OWNERSHIP CONCERN

The updated ownership creates the following structure:

    Castellan Holdings S.a.r.l. ──(70%)──> CastleTech BVI Ltd
                                                  ^
    Cerberus Consulting AG ─────(30%)─────────────┘
          |
    (Received EUR 3.3M FROM Castellan Holdings)

  This raises the question: did Castellan Holdings effectively fund the
  acquisition of a 30% stake in its own subsidiary by a third party?

  If so, this constitutes:
  - Potential dilution of group assets without UBO/board disclosure
  - Possible connected-party transaction in breach of fiduciary duties
  - Money laundering red flag: circular flow of funds designed to obscure
    the true nature of the transaction

4. SAR FILING DECISION

  DECISION: FILE SUSPICIOUS ACTIVITY REPORT

  Vote: UNANIMOUS (4-0, Koenig excused, Lehmann advisory/non-voting)

  Rationale: The cumulative pattern of undisclosed ownership changes,
  unexplained large-value transactions to a potentially shell entity,
  and the concurrent PEP criminal investigation collectively give rise
  to a reasonable suspicion that the transactions may involve the
  proceeds of criminal activity or may be connected to money laundering.

  The Committee specifically notes that no single factor alone would
  have triggered SAR filing. It is the ACCUMULATION and PATTERN that
  crosses the reasonable suspicion threshold.

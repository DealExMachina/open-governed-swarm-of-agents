3. EDD FINDINGS

3.1 Cerberus Consulting AG — Deep Dive
  - Incorporated 2024-03-19, Canton Zurich
  - Sole director and shareholder: Luca Ferretti (Italian, DOB 1969-07-22)
  - No employees registered with Swiss social insurance (SVA)
  - No website, no LinkedIn presence, no professional references
  - Office address (Bahnhofstrasse 42) is a serviced office / virtual
    address provider (Regus Switzerland GmbH)
  - Tax filings: none yet (too recently incorporated)
  - Banking relationship: Banque Cantonale de Zurich (confirmed)

  Assessment: Cerberus has NO demonstrable capability to deliver a
  EUR 4.5M AI technology platform. The entity appears to be a shell
  or pass-through vehicle.

3.2 Luca Ferretti — Background
  - Co-founded CastleTech Solutions with Viktor Castellan in 2009
  - Departed CastleTech in 2015 (2 years before the EUR 22M exit)
  - No public technology ventures since 2015
  - Resident in Zurich since 2018
  - No adverse media, no sanctions matches, no PEP status
  - CRITICAL: Viktor did NOT disclose the prior business relationship
    with Ferretti during onboarding or in the transaction explanation

3.3 Consulting Agreement Review
  - Agreement signed 2024-04-28 but backdated project start to 2024-03-01
  - Payment terms are heavily front-loaded: 60% paid in first 3 months
  - Intellectual property clause assigns all outputs to Castellan Holdings
  - No penalty clauses for non-delivery
  - No independent oversight or audit provisions
  - Fee benchmarking: EUR 4.5M for AI platform development is within
    market range for enterprise software BUT requires a substantial team.
    A sole-director company with no employees cannot credibly deliver.

3.4 Source of Funds for Cerberus Payments
  - Funds originated from Castellan Holdings operating account
  - Ultimately sourced from CastleTech BVI dividend (EUR 2.0M) and
    accumulated rental income
  - No unusual inflow pattern preceding the outflows

3.5 Cross-Reference with Marco Castellan (PEP)
  - No direct link between Marco Castellan and Cerberus Consulting AG
    identified at this time
  - However, the temporal proximity of Marco's trust addition (Oct 2023)
    and Cerberus incorporation (Mar 2024) is noted
  - Indirect benefit to Marco through trust assets cannot be excluded

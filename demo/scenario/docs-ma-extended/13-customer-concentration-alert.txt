1. PURPOSE

This memo provides an urgent update to the customer concentration analysis
(Document 04) following recent developments with NovaTech's largest customer.

2. REVISED CUSTOMER REVENUE DATA

Following the independent financial review (Document 11), customer ARR figures
have been restated based on adjusted revenue recognition. The revised top-5
customer breakdown is as follows:

  Rank  Customer                   Adjusted ARR    % of Adj. ARR   Change
  ----  --------                   ------------    -------------   ------
   1    Pharmakon GmbH              EUR 8.2M         21.6%         UP*
   2    BioVantage AG               EUR 4.8M         12.6%         DOWN
   3    MedCore Instruments         EUR 3.9M         10.3%         DOWN
   4    Helvetia Diagnostics        EUR 3.5M          9.2%         FLAT
   5    Rheingold Therapeutics      EUR 3.2M          8.4%         FLAT

  * Pharmakon's absolute ARR increased from EUR 7.8M to EUR 8.2M due to a
    mid-contract upsell in Q3 2025. However, the adjusted total ARR base
    (EUR 38.0M per Deloitte review) increases Pharmakon's concentration to
    21.6% — significantly above the 15.6% previously reported.

3. CRITICAL DEVELOPMENT: PHARMAKON CONTRACT STATUS

Through customer reference calls conducted during diligence, we have learned
the following material developments regarding Pharmakon GmbH:

  (a) Pharmakon's current contract expires June 30, 2026 — only 15 months
      from signing. The contract includes a 90-day termination-for-convenience
      clause (confirmed in Document 04).

  (b) Pharmakon's Head of Regulatory Affairs, Dr. Sabine Vogel, confirmed
      that Pharmakon is conducting a competitive evaluation of alternative
      platforms, including Veeva Systems RegulatoryOne and Rimsys.

  (c) The evaluation was triggered by Pharmakon's recent merger with
      HelixBio AG, which uses Veeva Systems for US regulatory submissions.
      The merged entity seeks platform consolidation across geographies.

  (d) Dr. Vogel stated: "We value our relationship with NovaTech but need
      to assess whether a single global platform would be more efficient
      for the combined organization."

4. IMPACT ASSESSMENT

  Scenario A (Pharmakon Renews):     No material impact
  Scenario B (Pharmakon Churns):     EUR 8.2M ARR loss (21.6% of base)
                                     Adjusted ARR falls to EUR 29.8M
                                     Implied valuation impact: EUR 60-80M

5. ADDITIONAL CONCENTRATION CONCERNS

With the adjusted ARR base, the top 3 customers now represent 44.5% of total
ARR. This exceeds the 40% threshold typically flagged in SaaS due diligence.

  Combined Top 3:    EUR 16.9M / EUR 38.0M = 44.5%
  Combined Top 5:    EUR 23.6M / EUR 38.0M = 62.1%
  Combined Top 10:   EUR 30.2M / EUR 38.0M = 79.5%

6. RECOMMENDED ACTIONS

  (a) Schedule executive-level meeting between Meridian and Pharmakon
  (b) Request NovaTech to present Pharmakon retention strategy
  (c) Model deal economics under both renewal and churn scenarios
  (d) Consider earn-out or holdback provisions tied to Pharmakon retention

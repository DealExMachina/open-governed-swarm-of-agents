1. VALUATION REVISION SUMMARY

Based on findings from Phase 2 diligence, the indicative valuation of NovaTech
AG has been revised materially downward from the initial EUR 420 million.

  Initial Indicative Valuation:     EUR 420 million
  Revised Valuation Range:          EUR 270 - 290 million
  Reduction:                        EUR 130 - 150 million (31-36%)
  Midpoint:                         EUR 280 million

2. KEY DRIVERS OF VALUATION ADJUSTMENT

  (a) Revenue Restatement (Document 11):
      ARR adjusted from EUR 50.0M to EUR 38.0M. At a revised multiple of
      7.0-7.5x ARR (reflecting lower growth profile and increased risk),
      the ARR-based valuation is EUR 266-285M.

  (b) IP Risk Discount:
      Patent disputes (Documents 12, 19) create estimated contingent
      liability of EUR 15-25M. Applied as a direct reduction to enterprise
      value: -EUR 15M at midpoint.

  (c) Key Person Risk (Document 14):
      CTO departure and associated talent flight risk. Estimated replacement
      and knowledge transfer cost: EUR 15-25M. Applied as EUR 10M discount
      (weighted probability).

  (d) Customer Concentration (Documents 13, 20):
      Pharmakon renewal at 15% discount plus performance exit clause.
      Revenue risk of EUR 2-3M annually. DCF impact: -EUR 12M.

  (e) Remediation Costs:
      - Cybersecurity remediation (Document 17):      EUR 0.5-0.7M
      - Regulatory compliance (Document 15):          EUR 1.0-1.6M
      - Technical debt (Document 14):                 EUR 3.0-5.0M
      - EU MDR compliance (if required):              EUR 1.5M
      - Infrastructure resilience (Document 18):      EUR 1.5-2.0M
      Total estimated remediation:                    EUR 7.5-10.8M

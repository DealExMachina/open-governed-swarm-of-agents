UPDATE: Pharmakon GmbH Contract Renewal Outcome

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Following three months of intensive negotiations (referenced in Document 13),
Pharmakon GmbH has agreed to a contract renewal with NovaTech AG. However, the
renewed terms represent materially different economics than the expiring
agreement.

2. RENEWED CONTRACT TERMS

  Contract Type:          Master Subscription Agreement (renewed)
  Effective Date:         July 1, 2026
  Term:                   2 years (expires June 30, 2028)
  Annual Contract Value:  EUR 6.97 million
  Prior ACV:              EUR 8.20 million
  Price Reduction:        15% (EUR 1.23M annual reduction)

3. KEY COMMERCIAL TERMS

  (a) Price Reduction: Pharmakon negotiated a 15% reduction in annual fees
      from EUR 8.20M to EUR 6.97M. This was driven by competitive pressure
      from Veeva Systems, which offered a consolidated global platform at
      a discount of approximately 25% vs. NovaTech's pricing.

  (b) Termination for Convenience: The renewed contract retains a
      termination-for-convenience clause, now with 180-day notice (increased
      from 90-day in the prior contract). This provides modestly improved
      revenue visibility.

  (c) Performance Exit Clause: NEW — Pharmakon has added a performance-based
      exit clause. If NovaTech's platform availability falls below 99.5% in
      any rolling 3-month period, or if response times exceed SLA thresholds
      for more than 72 consecutive hours, Pharmakon may terminate with 30-day
      notice and receive a pro-rata refund.

  (d) Scope Reduction: Pharmakon has excluded its newly acquired HelixBio AG
      subsidiary from the NovaTech contract. HelixBio will continue using
      Veeva Systems for US regulatory submissions and will migrate EU
      submissions to Veeva over the 2-year contract term.

  (e) Data Migration Clause: Pharmakon has negotiated a data portability
      clause requiring NovaTech to provide full data export in standard
      formats within 30 days of contract termination, at no additional cost.

4. FINANCIAL IMPACT

  Annual Revenue Impact:     -EUR 1.23M (from EUR 8.20M to EUR 6.97M)
  Two-Year Guaranteed Value: EUR 13.94M
  Risk of Mid-Term Exit:     MODERATE (performance clause creates pathway)

  Pharmakon's share of adjusted ARR changes:
    Prior:    EUR 8.20M / EUR 38.0M = 21.6%
    Renewed:  EUR 6.97M / EUR 38.0M = 18.3%

  Note: While the absolute concentration decreases, Pharmakon remains the
  largest customer by a significant margin.

5. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT

The renewal is a mixed outcome:

  POSITIVE:
  - Pharmakon retained as a customer for minimum 2 additional years
  - Relationship preserved at executive level
  - 180-day notice period provides better planning horizon

  NEGATIVE:
  - 15% price reduction sets a precedent for other contract renewals
  - Performance exit clause creates operational risk
  - HelixBio exclusion signals Pharmakon's long-term migration intent
  - Data portability clause facilitates eventual departure

6. IMPACT ON PROJECTIONS

The five-year projections (Document 09) assumed Pharmakon renewal at existing
rates. The EUR 1.23M annual reduction, combined with the HelixBio exclusion,
reduces projected FY2027 revenue by approximately EUR 2.5M when accounting
for foregone expansion revenue from the HelixBio subsidiary.

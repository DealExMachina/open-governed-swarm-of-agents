1. SCOPE

Argon Consulting was engaged to perform an independent technical due diligence
of NovaTech's RegAssure platform, including code quality analysis, architecture
review, and assessment of key person dependencies within the engineering team.

2. CRITICAL FINDING: CTO DEPARTURE

During confidential interviews with NovaTech's leadership team, Dr. Elena Voss
(CTO & Co-Founder) disclosed that she intends to resign effective Q4 2025,
regardless of the acquisition outcome. Dr. Voss cited personal reasons and
a desire to pursue academic research.

This directly contradicts management representations in Document 06, which
stated: "Dr. Voss has indicated her intent to remain through and beyond the
transition."

Furthermore, two senior engineers — Dr. Friedrich Wendt (Lead ML Scientist)
and Andrej Kozlov (Principal Backend Engineer) — have indicated they will follow
Dr. Voss to her next venture. Combined, these three individuals account for:

  - 4 of 7 granted patents (Voss named inventor)
  - 61% of commits to the ML pipeline repository (last 24 months)
  - Sole knowledge holders for the custom model training infrastructure
  - 8 of 18 ML team members have stated they would consider leaving if
    Dr. Voss and Dr. Wendt depart

3. CODE CONCENTRATION ANALYSIS

Our automated code analysis (using git-fame and custom tooling) reveals
concerning concentration in the codebase:

  Repository: regassure-ml-pipeline
    Dr. Elena Voss:       34% of all commits (last 24 months)
    Dr. Friedrich Wendt:  27% of all commits
    Combined:             61% of all commits
    Next contributor:     8% (Andrej Kozlov)

  Repository: regassure-core
    Andrej Kozlov:        22% of all commits
    Thomas Kessler:       18% of all commits
    Combined top 4:       58% of all commits

4. TECHNICAL DEBT FINDINGS

Contrary to the self-reported estimate of 12% technical debt (Document 05),
our assessment identifies significantly higher technical debt:

  (a) Legacy Ingestion Pipeline:  Built in 2019, uses deprecated Python 3.8
      patterns. Scheduled refactoring has been deferred three times.
      Estimated remediation: 6-9 months, 4 FTE engineers.

  (b) Test Coverage: Management reported >80%. Our analysis measured actual
      statement coverage at 54% for regassure-ml-pipeline and 71% for
      regassure-core. Integration test coverage is 38%.

  (c) Dependency Vulnerabilities: 23 npm packages and 14 Python packages
      with known CVEs, including 3 rated HIGH severity.

  (d) Documentation: API documentation covers only 40% of endpoints. Internal
      architecture documentation has not been updated since 2023.

5. DISASTER RECOVERY GAPS

  - Last successful DR test: November 2023 (14 months ago)
  - RTO target: 4 hours. Last measured RTO: estimated 12-16 hours
  - No documented runbook for ML model recovery
  - Backup verification is manual and performed quarterly

6. RISK QUANTIFICATION

  CTO Departure Impact:           HIGH — EUR 15-25M estimated cost to replace
                                  team and rebuild institutional knowledge
  Technical Debt Remediation:     EUR 3-5M over 18 months
  DR/Security Remediation:        EUR 1-2M over 12 months

7. RECOMMENDATIONS

  (a) Negotiate retention packages for Dr. Voss (minimum 24-month commitment)
  (b) Immediate knowledge transfer program for ML pipeline
  (c) Hire backup CTO / VP Engineering with ML expertise
  (d) Budget EUR 5-7M for technical debt and security remediation

1. OVERVIEW

This memorandum outlines the proposed settlement framework for resolving
the two outstanding IP disputes affecting NovaTech AG: (a) the Axion Corp
patent infringement lawsuit (Document 19), and (b) the Dr. Klaus Haber
co-ownership claims on EP3512644 and EP3847291 (Document 12).

2. AXION CORP SETTLEMENT

Following three rounds of mediation at the UPC (Munich Local Division),
the parties have reached the following settlement framework:

  (a) Financial Terms:
      - Lump-sum payment: EUR 1.5 - 2.0 million (to be finalized)
      - Ongoing royalty: 4% of net revenue attributable to the Predictive
        Risk Scoring module (estimated EUR 240K annually at current volumes)
      - Royalty term: 7 years or until expiration of EP4102883

  (b) Cross-License:
      - NovaTech grants Axion a non-exclusive license to EP3847291 for
        use outside the EU MDR compliance vertical
      - Axion grants NovaTech freedom-to-operate under EP4102883 for
        existing and future regulatory compliance products

  (c) Non-Assertion:
      - Mutual non-assertion covenant covering current product portfolios
      - 3-year standstill on new patent assertions between parties

  (d) Dismissal:
      - Axion withdraws infringement claims and injunction request
      - Each party bears its own legal costs

  Estimated Total Cost (Year 1): EUR 1.74 - 2.24 million
  Estimated Annual Ongoing Cost: EUR 240K (royalty, subject to growth)

3. DR. KLAUS HABER SETTLEMENT

Parallel negotiations with Dr. Haber's counsel (Rechtsanwalt Peter Lindner)
have produced the following proposed terms:

  (a) EP3847291 — Assignment Cure:
      - NovaTech pays the outstanding CHF 150,000 assignment consideration
        (as identified in Document 12) plus a premium of CHF 50,000 for
        the delay, totaling CHF 200,000
      - Dr. Haber executes a definitive, irrevocable assignment of all
        rights in EP3847291 to NovaTech AG

  (b) EP3512644 — Co-Ownership Resolution:
      - NovaTech acquires Dr. Haber's claimed co-ownership interest for
        EUR 600,000 - 800,000
      - Alternative: perpetual, royalty-free license from Dr. Haber to
        NovaTech for all applications of EP3512644

  (c) Consulting Engagement Termination:
      - Dr. Haber agrees to terminate his consulting arrangement with
        Axion Corp within 30 days of settlement
      - 24-month non-compete covering regulatory technology patent advisory

  (d) Mutual Release:
      - Full mutual release of all IP-related claims between NovaTech
        and Dr. Haber
      - Confidentiality obligations on settlement terms

  Total Haber Settlement Cost: EUR 800,000 - 1,200,000

4. COMBINED SETTLEMENT BUDGET

  Axion Settlement (lump sum):         EUR 1,500,000 - 2,000,000
  Axion Royalty (Year 1):              EUR   240,000
  Haber Assignment Cure:               CHF   200,000 (~EUR 210,000)
  Haber Co-Ownership Buyout:           EUR   600,000 -   800,000
  Legal Costs (both matters):          EUR   850,000
  ---------------------------------------------------------------
  Total Settlement Cost:               EUR 3,400,000 - 4,100,000

5. TIMELINE

  Haber Settlement:     Target execution by July 31, 2025
  Axion Settlement:     Target execution by August 31, 2025
  UPC Case Dismissal:   Within 30 days of Axion settlement
  Cross-License Effective: Upon Axion settlement execution

6. IMPACT ON ACQUISITION

The settlement framework, if executed, substantially reduces IP risk:
  - Eliminates the EUR 15M damages claim from Axion
  - Resolves co-ownership disputes on both affected patents
  - Provides freedom-to-operate certainty for the Predictive Risk module
  - Removes Dr. Haber as a competitive threat for 24 months

Residual IP risk post-settlement: LOW (reduced from HIGH)

7. RECOMMENDATION

We recommend proceeding with both settlements as outlined. The combined cost
of EUR 3.4-4.1M is significantly below the worst-case litigation exposure
of EUR 15-25M and eliminates the risk of injunctive relief that could
disable a revenue-generating module used by 31 customers.

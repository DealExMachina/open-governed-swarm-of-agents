1. RISK ASSESSMENT FRAMEWORK

This preliminary risk assessment evaluates NovaTech AG across six dimensions
using a standardized LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH rating scale. This assessment is based
on management-provided information and will be validated during Phase 2 deep
dive diligence.

2. RISK SUMMARY MATRIX

  Dimension                    Rating    Confidence    Notes
  ---------                    ------    ----------    -----
  Financial Performance        LOW       Medium        Strong growth, unaudited
  Intellectual Property        LOW       High          Clean FTO opinion, 7 patents
  Customer Concentration       LOW       Medium        Top client 15.6% of ARR
  Technology & Product         LOW       High          Modern stack, good velocity
  Regulatory & Compliance      LOW       High          SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR clean
  Team & Key Person            LOW       Medium        Founders committed, low churn

  OVERALL ASSESSMENT:          LOW RISK — PROCEED TO FULL DILIGENCE

3. DETAILED FINDINGS

  3.1 Financial Performance
  Management-reported figures show compelling growth (45% 3yr CAGR) with a clear
  path to profitability by Q3 2026. Unit economics are strong (LTV/CAC 5.2x).
  Note: figures are unaudited and will require independent verification.

  3.2 Intellectual Property
  Seven granted patents provide meaningful defensive IP. FTO opinion from
  reputable counsel confirms no infringement risk. Two pending PCT applications
  strengthen future position. All IP cleanly assigned to NovaTech AG.

  3.3 Customer Concentration
  Top customer Pharmakon GmbH represents 15.6% of ARR. While above the 10%
  threshold for concentration risk, the relationship is long-standing (since
  2019) and multi-year contracted. No single customer represents existential
  risk. Logo retention at 96% is excellent.

  3.4 Technology & Product
  Modern cloud-native architecture on AWS with strong engineering practices.
  Technical debt estimated at 12% (manageable). CTO Dr. Elena Voss provides
  strong technical leadership. No material scalability concerns identified.

  3.5 Regulatory & Compliance
  Clean SOC 2 Type II report, ISO 27001 certification, and GDPR compliance.
  EU MDR assessment confirms platform is out-of-scope (with minor caveats).
  No pending regulatory matters in any jurisdiction.

  3.6 Team & Key Person
  Both founders are committed to remaining post-acquisition. VP Engineering
  under 2-year retention agreement. Employee turnover (11%) below industry
  average. No material key person risk identified.

4. RECOMMENDATION

Based on this preliminary assessment, the Investment Committee recommends
proceeding to full due diligence with the following priority areas:

  (a) Independent financial audit (engage Deloitte or KPMG)
  (b) Technical architecture deep dive by external consultants
  (c) Customer reference calls (minimum 5 top-10 accounts)
  (d) IP deep dive with independent patent counsel

No deal-breaking risks have been identified at this stage.

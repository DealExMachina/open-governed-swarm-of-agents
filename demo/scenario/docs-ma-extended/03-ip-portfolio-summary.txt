2. FREEDOM-TO-OPERATE OPINION

Bauer & Richter LLP conducted a freedom-to-operate (FTO) analysis covering
NovaTech's core product suite. The FTO opinion, dated 2024-12-01, concludes
that NovaTech's products do not infringe any known third-party patents in the
European regulatory technology space. No blocking patents were identified.

3. TRADE SECRETS & KNOW-HOW

NovaTech maintains proprietary training datasets comprising 2.4 million
regulatory documents spanning 14 EU jurisdictions. All employees and contractors
have executed invention assignment and non-disclosure agreements.

4. IP OWNERSHIP CONFIRMATION

All patents listed above are solely owned by NovaTech AG. Invention assignment
agreements are on file for all named inventors, including former employees.
Dr. Klaus Haber departed NovaTech in March 2024 and signed a separation
agreement confirming full IP assignment.

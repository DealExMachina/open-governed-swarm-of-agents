2. EU MEDICAL DEVICE REGULATION (MDR) STATUS

NovaTech's RegAssure platform is classified as a "Qualified Infrastructure"
tool under EU MDR — it assists customers in preparing regulatory submissions
but is not itself classified as a medical device or Software as Medical Device
(SaMD).

An initial assessment conducted by TUV SUD (November 2024) confirmed that
RegAssure does not fall under the scope of EU MDR 2017/745 as a standalone
product. However, certain modules (specifically the Predictive Risk Scoring
module) may require additional assessment under MDR Annex VIII Rule 11 if
deployed in clinical decision-support contexts.

  MDR Readiness Assessment:        COMPLETE
  MDR Classification:              Out-of-scope (with caveats per Rule 11)
  Estimated Remediation (if needed): EUR 1.5 million over 12 months

3. SWISS REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

NovaTech AG, as a Swiss entity, complies with the Swiss Federal Act on Data
Protection (FADP/nDSG) as revised September 2023. The company is registered
with the Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC).

Cross-border data transfers between Switzerland and the EU are conducted under
the EU-Swiss Data Protection Adequacy Decision. All data processing activities
are documented in the Record of Processing Activities (ROPA).

FINMA (Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority) oversight is not applicable
as NovaTech does not provide financial services. The company has confirmed
compliance with all applicable Swiss commercial regulations.

4. GERMAN SUBSIDIARY COMPLIANCE

NovaTech GmbH (Munich) operates under German employment law and commercial
regulations. The subsidiary maintains its own works council consultation
processes as required under the Betriebsverfassungsgesetz (BetrVG). All German
employment contracts comply with the Arbeitszeitengesetz (ArbZG) and applicable
collective bargaining agreements.

5. PENDING REGULATORY MATTERS

No pending regulatory investigations, enforcement actions, or material
compliance deficiencies have been identified across any jurisdiction.

6. ANTI-CORRUPTION & SANCTIONS

NovaTech maintains an anti-bribery and corruption (ABC) policy consistent with
Swiss law and the UK Bribery Act 2010 (applicable to the Dublin subsidiary's
UK-facing activities). Sanctions screening is performed quarterly. No issues
have been identified.

CONFIDENTIAL — PROJECT HORIZON
Board Resolution: Conditional Approval of NovaTech AG Acquisition
Date: 2025-07-25
Prepared by: Secretary to the Board — Meridian Capital Partners
Attendees: Full Board of Directors (7 members present, quorum achieved)

1. RESOLUTION

The Board of Directors of Meridian Capital Partners, having reviewed all
due diligence materials (Documents 01-23) and the revised valuation analysis
(Document 21), hereby APPROVES the acquisition of NovaTech AG subject to the
conditions set forth below.

2. APPROVED TRANSACTION TERMS

  Purchase Price:              EUR 280 million (enterprise value, cash-free
                               debt-free basis)
  Escrow / Holdback:           EUR 25 million (held for 24 months post-close)
  Earn-Out:                    Up to EUR 30 million, contingent on:
                               - FY2026 ARR >= EUR 45 million: EUR 15M
                               - FY2027 ARR >= EUR 55 million: EUR 15M
  Key Employee Retention Pool: EUR 15 million (36-month vesting)
  Maximum Total Consideration: EUR 350 million

3. PRE-SIGNING CONDITIONS (Must be satisfied before SPA execution)

  Condition 1: IP Resolution
    Both IP settlements (Axion Corp and Dr. Klaus Haber) must be fully
    executed with all payments made and all assignments recorded at the
    European Patent Office. The Axion UPC case must be formally dismissed.
    Deadline: September 30, 2025

  Condition 2: CTO Transition Plan
    A binding CTO transition plan must be in place, including:
    (a) Identification and engagement of a successor CTO candidate
    (b) Dr. Elena Voss commitment to a minimum 12-month transition period
        (with appropriate retention incentive, funded from the EUR 15M pool)
    (c) Knowledge transfer plan covering all 4 patents where Dr. Voss is
        named inventor, the ML pipeline architecture, and the custom model
        training infrastructure
    Deadline: September 30, 2025

  Condition 3: Cybersecurity Remediation
    All three critical vulnerabilities identified in the penetration test
    (Document 17) must be remediated and verified by CyberEdge GmbH.
    Specifically:
    (a) CVE-2025-NOVA-001 (SQL injection) — patched and verified
    (b) CVE-2025-NOVA-002 (broken access control) — patched and verified
    (c) CVE-2025-NOVA-003 (exposed AWS credentials) — rotated and verified,
        with CloudTrail audit confirming no unauthorized access
    Deadline: August 31, 2025

4. POST-CLOSING COVENANTS

  The Board further requires that the following be addressed within 12 months
  of closing:

  (a) FINMA compliance remediation (Document 15): complete within 6 months
  (b) Swiss data transfer compliance (Document 15): complete within 3 months
  (c) EU MDR transition initiated (Document 23): Phase 1 within 6 months
  (d) Disaster recovery testing (Document 18): complete within 3 months
  (e) German works council process (Document 16): initiate consultation
  (f) OpenAI dependency migration (Document 18): plan within 6 months

5. VALUATION RATIONALE

The Board acknowledges the reduction from the initial EUR 420M indicative
valuation to EUR 280M, driven by:
  - Revenue overstatement (24% ARR reduction)
  - IP litigation risk (now substantially mitigated by settlements)
  - Key person risk (CTO departure)
  - Customer concentration (Pharmakon at 21.6%)
  - Required remediation investments (~EUR 10M)

The EUR 280M price represents 7.4x adjusted ARR, which the Board considers
fair given the risk profile and comparable transactions.

6. AUTHORIZATION

The Board authorizes Marcus Reinhardt (Head of Corporate Development) and
external counsel (Lenz & Staehelin, Zurich) to proceed with SPA negotiation
on the terms approved herein. Any material deviation from these terms
requires Board re-approval.

Vote: 6 in favor, 1 abstention (Dr. Petra Hoffmann, citing residual IP risk)

Approved by the Board of Directors, Meridian Capital Partners

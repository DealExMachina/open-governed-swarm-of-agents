1. MARKET OVERVIEW

The European Regulatory Technology (RegTech) market for life sciences is valued
at EUR 2.1 billion (2025) and projected to reach EUR 5.7 billion by 2030,
representing an 18.3% CAGR. Growth drivers include:

  - EU MDR/IVDR transition deadlines creating compliance urgency
  - Increasing regulatory complexity across EU member states
  - Digitalization mandates from EMA and national competent authorities
  - AI adoption in regulatory affairs departments

2. NOVATECH MARKET POSITION

  Current ARR:                EUR 50.0 million
  Estimated Market Share:     2.4% of addressable market
  Target Segment:             Mid-to-large enterprises (500+ employees)
  Geographic Focus:           DACH, Nordics, Southern Europe

NovaTech is positioned as a Tier 2 player in the European RegTech market,
behind established incumbents but ahead of emerging point-solution providers.

3. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

  Tier 1 (>EUR 200M revenue):
    - Veeva Systems (US) — Dominant in pharma regulatory. EUR 2.4B global rev.
    - IQVIA RegulatoryOne — Part of larger data/analytics offering
    - MasterControl — Broad quality + regulatory suite

  Tier 2 (EUR 20-100M revenue):
    - NovaTech AG (Target) — EUR 50M ARR, EU MDR specialization
    - Rimsys (US) — Medical device regulatory focus, EUR 35M ARR
    - Compliance.ai (US) — AI-powered regulatory change management

  Tier 3 (<EUR 20M revenue):
    - RegDesk — Emerging competitor, raised Series B in 2024
    - Kolabtree — Niche consultancy/platform hybrid
    - Celegence — India-based, cost-competitive

4. COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES

  (a) EU MDR Specialization: NovaTech is the only Tier 2 player with deep
      native support for EU MDR 2017/745, including Annex mapping, EUDAMED
      integration, and post-market surveillance automation.

  (b) Multi-Language NLP: RegAssure processes regulatory documents in 14
      European languages, a capability not matched by US-centric competitors.

  (c) Data Network Effects: 4.2 TB of indexed regulatory documents create a
      compounding advantage in ML model accuracy.

  (d) European Data Residency: All processing within EU/Switzerland, critical
      for regulated industries with data sovereignty requirements.

5. COMPETITIVE THREATS

  - Veeva Systems expanding EU MDR module (expected GA in Q3 2026)
  - Axion Corp (Munich) has filed patent applications in adjacent areas and
    has publicly announced intent to enter the MDR compliance automation space
  - Open-source regulatory tools emerging from academic projects
  - Large consulting firms (Deloitte, McKinsey) building proprietary tools

6. WIN/LOSS ANALYSIS (Last 12 Months)

  Competitive Win Rate:       62% (vs. 57% prior year)
  Most Frequent Competitor:   Veeva Systems (lost 8 of 22 contested deals)
  Primary Win Factor:         EU MDR depth, deployment speed, pricing
  Primary Loss Factor:        Brand recognition, global coverage

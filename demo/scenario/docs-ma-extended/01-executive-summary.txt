1. TRANSACTION OVERVIEW

Meridian Capital Partners ("Meridian" or "Acquirer") proposes the acquisition of
NovaTech AG ("NovaTech" or "Target"), a Zurich-headquartered SaaS company
specializing in regulatory compliance automation for the European life sciences
sector. The indicative offer is EUR 420 million on a cash-free, debt-free basis,
representing approximately 8.4x trailing annual recurring revenue (ARR).

NovaTech was founded in 2017 by Dr. Lukas Meier (CEO) and Dr. Elena Voss (CTO).
The company is incorporated under Swiss law (Handelsregister Kanton Zurich,
CHE-114.637.829) with wholly-owned subsidiaries in Germany (NovaTech GmbH,
Munich) and Ireland (NovaTech Europe Ltd, Dublin).

2. STRATEGIC RATIONALE

The acquisition positions Meridian to enter the EUR 2.1 billion European
regulatory technology market, which is projected to grow at 18.3% CAGR through
2030. NovaTech's platform addresses EU MDR (Medical Device Regulation) and IVDR
compliance, a segment experiencing acute demand following the May 2024 transition
deadline.

3. OWNERSHIP STRUCTURE

NovaTech AG share capital comprises 1,000,000 registered shares (CHF 1.00 par).

  - Cash on Hand:           EUR 14.5 million

5. INDICATIVE TIMELINE

  Phase 1 — Initial Extraction (Weeks 1-4):    Data room review, management meetings
  Phase 2 — Deep Dive Diligence (Weeks 4-8):   Financial audit, IP review, regulatory
  Phase 3 — Negotiation & Close (Weeks 8-18):  SPA drafting, regulatory approvals, signing

6. RECOMMENDATION

Based on preliminary analysis, the Investment Committee recommends proceeding to
full due diligence. NovaTech represents a compelling growth asset with strong
product-market fit, defensible IP, and a proven management team. Risk factors
are assessed as LOW across all dimensions at this preliminary stage.
